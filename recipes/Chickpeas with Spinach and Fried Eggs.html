Chickpeas with Spinach and Fried Eggs

2 Servings

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 garlic cloves, thinly sliced
  • 200g fresh spinach, coarsely chopped (or frozen spinach)
  • salt
  • pepper
  • 1 small chopped onion
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/4 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 2 cans chickpeas
  • 1 can chopped tomatoes
  • 1 stock cube
  • 1 cup of water
  • 4 large eggs

Heat 1 tablespoon oil over medium heat. Add 1 garlic clove and stir for 1-2 minutes. Add the spinach, season with salt and pepper and cook until slightly wilted. Set aside and wipe out pan.

Heat another tablespoon of oil and add the rest of the garlic and onions once hot. Cook until onion is softened, 3–4 minutes. Add cumin and paprika and stir for 1–2 minutes. Add chickpeas and tomatoes, stir everytng and cook until nearly dry, 8–10 minutes.

Add stockcube and a cup of water, then cook on medium heat for another 10 minutes. Once done, add the spinach and mix well.

Fry the eggs in another pan once the chickpeas are nearly done. Spread chickpeas and spinach on a plate and serve the eggs on top.
