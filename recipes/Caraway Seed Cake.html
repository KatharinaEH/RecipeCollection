Caraway Seed Cake

1 small loaf tin

  • 175g self-raising flour, sifted
  • 175g margarine
  • 175g golden caster sugar
  • 3 large eggs, beaten
  • 3 tablespoons milk
  • 3 rounded teaspoons caraway seeds
  • For the topping:
    • 1 level tablespoons demerara sugar
    • 1 level tablespoon flaked almonds

Preheat the oven to 180C.

Sift the flour into a roomy mixing bowl, lifting the sieve quite high to give the flour a good airing as it goes down, then simply add all the other ingredients.

Whisk everything for about 1 minute until you have a smooth creamy consistency.

Prepare a loaf tin with baking paper, then spoon the mixture into it. Level off the surface, then sprinkle the demerara sugar and crushed almonds all over.

Bake on a lower shelf for about 65 minutes, or until the cake is springy in the centre. Cool in the tin for 10 minutes, then turn out onto a wire rack to cool.
