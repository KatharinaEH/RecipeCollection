Roasted Beet and Potato Soup

2 Servings

  • For the Soup:
    • 2 small beetroots
    • 4 medium potatoes
    • juice of 1 lemon
    • 1 teaspoon chilli flakes
    • 1 teaspoon gorund cumin
    • 2 garlic cloves, crushed
    • 2 teaspoons ground coriander
    • 270ml coconut milk
  • For the Croutons:
    • 4 medium potatoes
    • olive oil
    • 2 teaspoons chilli flakes
    • salt and pepper

Preheat the oven to 220ï¿½C. Place the beetroor, skin on, on a baking tray and roast for about 1 hour, or until there are bubbles under the skin.

Meanwhile, peel the potatoes for the soup, place them in a steamer, and steam for 40 min, until really soft.

To make the croutons, chop the potatoes (no need to peel) into small, bite-sized pieces and place them in a baking tray with olive oil plus the chilli flakes, salt and pepper. Bake for 45 minutes, until crispy outside and tender within.

When the beetroot is cool enough to handle, peel the skin. Chop it in half and place it in a pan. Add the steamed potato, lemon juice, chilli flakes, cumin, garlic, coriander and coconut milk, plus some salt and pepper. Blend with a handblender until smooth. If it is too thick, add some water.

Heat the soup up again, then place in bowl and sprinkle with the roast potato croutons.
