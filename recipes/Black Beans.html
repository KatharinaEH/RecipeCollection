Black Beans

100g dried beans = 230g cooked beans = 1 can drained beans

  • 100g dried black beans
  • 1 small onion
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 600ml water

Peel the onion and the garlic cloves.

Put the dried black beans, onion, garlic and salt in a bowl and add about 600ml cold water.

Bring to a rolling boil over high heat, then cook for 10 min.

Reduce the heat to low and simmer for another 60 min. Refill water if necessary.

After 60 min try the beans and if they are soft, put them on high heat, until all the water is evaporated.

Source:

The Lazy Cook's Way to Great Black Beans
