Garlic Sautéd Beans and Mushrooms

2 Servings

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 400g can of cannellini beans, drained and rinsed (or any other beans)
  • 12 chestnut mushrooms
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 tablespoon dried mixed herbs
  • salt and pepper

Slice the mushrooms, then sauté them in the olive oil with the garlic, herbs, salt and pepper.

After 3-4 minutes add the beans, then leave until everyhing is hot and the mushrooms are slightly browned.

Serve with:

Sweet Potato and Coconut Soup

Pumpkin Soup
