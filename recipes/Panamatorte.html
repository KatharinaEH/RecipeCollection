Panamatorte

1 cake

  • cake:
    • 100g caster sugar
    • 5 eggs (+ leftover egg white form creme)
    • 100g cooking chocolate, grated
    • 100g ground almonds
  • creme:
    • 100g margarine
    • 80g icing sugar
    • 90g cooking chocolate
    • 1 egg yolk
  • 50g roasted almond slivers for decoration

Preheat the oven to 170C.

Make a really stiff meringue out of the egg whites and a bit of the sugar.

Whip the egg yolks and the sugar until creamy, then add the grated chocolate and almonds.

Carefully mix the meringue into the mass, fill it in two round cake tins and then bake for around 20 minutes.

For the creme, melt the chocolate. Mix the margarine and sugar until very creamy. Add the egg yolks one at a time, mixing well in between. Add the melted chocolate once it has cooled enough.

After the cake has cooled, spread a third of the creme in between the two layers, then put the rest around the cake. Press the almonds into the creme for decoration.
