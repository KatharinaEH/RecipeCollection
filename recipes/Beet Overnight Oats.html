Sweet Beet Overnight Oats

3 Servings

  • 1 cooked beetroot
  • 1 apple
  • 1 tablespoon maple syryp
  • 1 tablespoon peanut butter
  • 1 banana
  • 140g oats
  • 2 tablespoons chia seeds

Chop the beetroot, the apple and banana and put them in a bowl.

Add 200ml of water, the maple syrup and peanut butter and blend until smooth.

Put the oats into a bowl, pour the mixture over it, then put the chia seeds on top.

Soak for a minimum of 4 hours, best overnight.

Source:

Deliciously Ella - Every Day - Ella Woodward
