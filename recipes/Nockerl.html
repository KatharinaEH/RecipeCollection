Nockerl

6 Servings

  • 500g flour
  • 500ml milk
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 1 tablespoon salt

Mix the milk and the eggs. Add the salt and oil, then the flour and beat in for about 5 minutes, until little bubbles of air are coming to the surface.

Prepare a big pot of boiling water, then add the mixture through a Nockerlsieb or by cutting off little bits over the edge of a chopping board.

Cook for 2-3 minutes, until the Nockerl are swimming on the surface, then take out with a slotted spoon.

Rinse well with cold water to keep from sticking together.

Serve with:

Fried White Cabbage

Goulash
