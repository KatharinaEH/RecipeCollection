Rhubarb Crumble

8 Servings

  • Crumble:
    • 300g self-raising flour
    • 200g margarine (cold)
    • 100g caster sugar
    • pinch of salt
  • Rhubarb Filling:
    • 400g rhubarb
    • 50g caster sugar
    • 1 teaspoons cornflour

  • Alternative Fillings:
    • Apple & Blackberry Filling:
      • 400g apples
      • 125g blackberries
      • 50g caster sugar
      • 1 teaspoons cornflour
    • Apple & Strawberry Filling:
      • 400g apples
      • 250g strawberries
      • 50g caster sugar
      • 1 teaspoons cornflour
    • Raspberry & Strawberry Filling:
      • 300g raspberries
      • 300g strawberries
      • 50g caster sugar
      • 1 teaspoons cornflour

Preheat oven 160°C.

Cube the butter, toss with the flour and cut it with a kinfe until the butter is in small pieces. Then rub the butter into the flour with your fingertips until the mixture resembles breadcrumbs. Stir in the sugar and a pinch of salt. Store in fridge until you need it.

Cut the rhubarb into pieces and toss with the sugar and cornflour.

Oil the pie dish and then pour in the rhubarb. It will shrink when cooked, so pile it up a bit.

Pour the crumble mixture over the rhubarb without pressing down, then bake for about an hour, until bubbling and nicely coloured.

Serve with:

Custard
