Kimchijeon (Kimchi Pancakes)

2 Servings

  • 1 cup of chopped kimchi
  • 3 tablespoons of kimchi juice
  • 2 tablespoons chopped onion
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/2 cup flour (all purpose flour)
  • 1/4 cup of water

Mix all the ingredients together.

Heat up a pan to medium-high heat and add some frying oil.

Fry the pancakes for about 1.5 minutes on each side, until crsipy, then turn over.

Fry until crispy on both sides, then serve straight away.
