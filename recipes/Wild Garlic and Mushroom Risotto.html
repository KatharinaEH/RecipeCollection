Wild Garlic and Mushroom Risotto

2 Servings

  • 160g risotto rice (or shortgrain, paella, ...)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 small onion
  • 125ml white wine
  • 500-700ml vegetalbe broth
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 200g mushrooms
  • 1 teaspoon dried herbs
  • pepper
  • 1 handfull of wild garlic (about 50g)
  • wild garlic flowers (for decoration)
  • parmesan (optional)

Dice the onion and heat it in a tablespoon of olive oil over medium heat for about 5 minutes.

Add the rice, cook until slightly glassy, then add the white wine. Add the broth a little at a time, while stirring the rice occassionally.

While the risotto cooks, slice the garlic and mushrooms. Heat them in a pan, until all the water is gone, season with mixed herbs and pepper, then put to the side until serving.

Slice the wild garlic and add to the risotto about 5 minutes before it is done. Serve the risotto with mushrooms on top and decorate with the flowers and optionally paremsan.
