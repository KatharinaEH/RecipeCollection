Pea and Cauliflower Bowl

2 Serving

  • 1 small head of cauliflower
  • 1 tsp ground turmeric
  • 1 tsp chilli flakes
  • Salt and pepper
  • Olive oil
  • 1 bunch green asparagus
  • 150g kale
  • 200g frozen peas
  • Juice of 1/2 lime

Preheat the oven to 200C.

Chop the cauliflower into florets and place these on a large baking tray (with space for the asparagus and kale) with the turmeric, chilli flakes, lots of black pepper, a little salt and a drizzling of olive oil. Put in the oven to cook for 25 minutes.

After 10 minutes, add the asparagus to the tray.

Finally, five to eight minutes before the end, put the torn-up kale leaves on the tray too.

Meanwhile, put the peas in a saucepan of boiling water, allowing them to cook for 5-10 minutes. Drain them and place in a bowl with black pepper, salt, the lime juice and one tablespoon of olive oil, then mash the peas.

Arrange the baked veggies on the bed of pea mash to serve.
