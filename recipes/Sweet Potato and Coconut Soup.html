Sweet Potato and Coconut Soup

3 Servings

  • 2 sweet potatoes
  • 400ml can of coconut milk
  • 10g fresh coriander leaves
  • 4 teaspoons soy sauce / tamari
  • 2 teaspoons paprika
  • 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
  • 2 teaspoon apple cider vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds

Peel the sweet potatos, chop them into bite-sized chunks and place them in a steamer. Steam them for about 45 minutes, until they're really soft.

Blend the sweet potatoes with the remaining ingredients and 200ml of water until smooth.

Heat the soup gently until it's nice and warm.

Serve with:

Garlic Sauted Beans and Mushrooms

Sweet Potato Wedges
