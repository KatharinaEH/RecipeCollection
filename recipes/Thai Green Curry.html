Thai Green Curry

4 Servings

  • Curry Paste:
    • 4 cloves garlic
    • 2 shallots or 1 onion
    • 5 cm piece of ginger
    • 2 stalks lemongrass
    • 4 green chillies
    • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
    • 1/2 bunch fresh coriander
    • 1/2 bunch fresh basil
    • 1 tablespoon fish sauce
    • 1/2 tabelspoon vegetable oil
  • 250g new potatoes
  • 125g french beans
  • 1 tablespoon coconut oil
  • 1 can coconut milk (try 2 next time)
  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 1 lime (optional)

Peel the garlic, onion and ginger and chop roughly. Chop the lemongrass and chillies, then put all the ingredients for the curry paste in a blender and blend well.

Half the new potatoes, then cook them for 10 minutes. Add the green beans for the last 4 minutes.

In the meantime, chop the chicken into bite-sized pieces.

Heat a pan over high heat, add coconut oil and the curry paste and heat for 30 seconds. Then add the coconut milk and bring to a boil. Once it is hot, boil the chicken in it for about 8 minutes.

Once the chicken is done, add the potatoes and the french beans, then serve with rice or naan bread.

Serve with:

Naan Bread, Rice
