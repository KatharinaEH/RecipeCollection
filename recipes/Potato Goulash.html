Potato Goulash

4 Servings

  • 15 medium potatoes
  • 3 onions
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons noble-sweet paprika
  • 1/2 litre water
  • 2 pinches marjoram
  • 1 bayleave
  • 4 teaspoons salt
  • 3 teaspoons stock powder (replacable by salt)
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste (optional)
  • 1 dash apple cider vinegar
  • smoky or spicy sausage (optional; Kabernossi, Puszta)

Chop onion and roast over medium heat until golden. Add paprika, mix well and cover with water.

In the meantime, peel and cube the the potatoes, then add them to the onion mix. Cover everthing with water and add the majoran, salt, stock, onion and vinegar.

Boil on low-medium heat until the potatoes are done, about 39 min. If required, add some water, so the potatoes are coverd at all times.

Add the sausage if using any 5 minutes before the potatoes are done. If required mash some of the potatoes to get a creamier consistency.
