Fried White Cabbage

4 Servings

  • 1/2 head white cabbage
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon caraway seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 50g bacon (optional)

Quarter the cabbage and then cut into 2 cm slices.

Heat the oil in a big pan, add the caraway seeds and fry for 30 seconds. If using the bacon, slice it and add it too.

Add the cabbage and about 2cm of water, to keep it from burning, then put a lid on. Add more water whenever needed.

When the cabbage is soft, add salt to taste.

Serve with:

Nockerl (cooked in the cabbage for last 10 min)
