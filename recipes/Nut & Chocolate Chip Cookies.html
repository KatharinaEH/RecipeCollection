Nut & Chocolate Chip Cookies

20 cookies

  • 125g butter
  • 65g soft brown sugar
  • 85g castor sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla essence
  • 250g self raising flour
  • 175g chopped nuts (brazil, pecan, hazelnut, ...)
  • 100g dark choc chips
  • 1-2 tablespoons milk

Preheat oven to 170Â°C.

Cream together butter, sugar, egg and vanilla and beat until creamy.

Stir in flour. Add nuts and choc chips. If necessary, add the milk.

Roll into balls and space out evenly on trays, allow room for spreading.

Bake for 12 minutes or until golden and let cool on the baking tray for 5 minutes.
