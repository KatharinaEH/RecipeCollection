Celery Soup

4 Servings

  • 1 onion
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1 teaspoon thyme
  • 1 head celery
  • 2 cups cauliflower (or optionally potato)
  • 1 litre vegetable stock

Chop the onion and garlic and sautee in olive oil until soft.

Wash the celery and cauliflower and chop roughly.

Add all the remaining ingredients to the onion, then simmer for about 20 minutes.

Puree the soup with a hand blender. Taste and season with salt and pepper, if necessary.

Serve with:

Fried croutons

Breadrolls
