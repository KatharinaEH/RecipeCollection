Fried Gnocchi With Garlic Mushrooms

2 Servings

  • 500g gnocchi
  • 250g mushrooms
  • 1 chicken breast
  • olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 100g peas
  • salt & pepper

Boil a pot of water. Add the peas and as soon as they are done, put them on an extra plate.

Cut the chicken breast into bite-sized pieces, then fry in a tablespoon of olive oil. Put it to the side, as soon as it has some color.

Slice the garlic, then quarter the mushrooms. Fry them for 5 minutes in some oil, then put to the side with the chicken.

Boil some more water, heat the gnocchi, and when they are done, fry them until they go slightly crispy and get some colour.

Add the peas, chicken and mushrooms to the gnocchi and stir well.
