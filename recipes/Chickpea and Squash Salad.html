Chickpea and Squash Salad

2 Servings

  • Salad:
    • 500g/ 1/2 butternut squash
    • 2 teaspoons paprika
    • 2 teaspoons dried mixed herbs
    • salt and pepper
    • olive oil
    • 1 can chickpeas, drained and rinsed
    • 1 teaspoon chili powder
    • 2 big handfuls rocket
    • 40g sun-dried tomatoes, soaked in water and chopped
  • Dressing:
    • 1 tablespoon olive oil
    • 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
    • 1/2 teaspoon honey
    • 1 teaspoon tumeric
    • salt and pepper

Preheat the oven to 200C.

Peel the squash, then cut it into bite-sized pieces. Place on a baking tray with the paprika, mixed herbs, a little salt and olive oil. Bake for about 30 minutes, until tender.

Place the chickpeas on a separate baking tray with the chilli powder, toss well to coat and bake for 15 minutes, until they're firm but not too crunchy.

Mix all the dressing ingredients together, seasoning with a bit of salt and lots of pepper.

Once the chickpeas and squash have cooked, pour the dressing over the rocket, then arrange the rocket, chickpeas, squash and sundried tomatoes on a plate.
