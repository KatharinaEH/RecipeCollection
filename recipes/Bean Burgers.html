Bean Burgers

8 Patties

  • 2 cans kidney beans
  • 3 carrots
  • 1 onion
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 1 egg (optional)
  • 30-40g of breadcrumbs or flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 2 tablespoons oil for frying

Dice the onion and garlic finely, grate the carrot, add the egg and mix all with the drained beans.

Mix in the salt and spices, then puree or mash until about half of it is mashed and add the flour.

Form into patties, then chill for half an hour to firm up.

Fry on medium heat on both sides for about 10 minutes each, until golden brown and warmed through.

Serve with:

Burger bun, lettuce, salsa, avocado, tomato, melted cheese, ...
