Paella

4 Servings

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 4 chicken breast
  • 100g chorizo
  • 1 onion
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 4 peppers (mixed)
  • 200g peas
  • 300g rice
  • broth
  • 1 teaspoon tumeric
  • 1 teaspoon noble sweet paprika

Preheat oven to 180Â°C.

Chop the onion, garlic and peppers, except for a dozen pieces of pepper for decoration.

Heat up a ovenproof pan over high heat and frie the chicken breast just until it has a bit of colour. Then take it out and do the same with the chorizo.

Fry the onion and garlic in the same pan, then add the peppers and peas. After about 5 min, add the rice, tumeric and chorizo, mix everything well, then cover with broth.

Arrange the chicken and remaining pepper on top, then cook in the oven for about 40-50 min.

Optionally add some fish, squid or prawns.
