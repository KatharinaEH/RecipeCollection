Chocolate Paste Icing

  • 1-2 part unsalted butter
  • 1 part cocoa
  • 5 parts icing sugar
  • milk as needed
  • pinch salt

Bring the butter to roomtemperature. Sieve the cocoa over the butter and mix well with a handblender.

Sieve the sugar into it, mix and add milk per tablespoon as needed.
