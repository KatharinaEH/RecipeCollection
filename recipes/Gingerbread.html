Gingercake

10"x13" tray

  • 250g (8.8 oz) margarine plus 2 teaspoons
  • 160ml (5.5 fl. oz) black treacle
  • 100ml (3.3 fl. oz) golden syrup
  • 250g (8.8 oz) dark brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 375g (13.2 oz) self raising flour
  • 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 2.5 teaspoons baking powder
  • 2 tablespoons ground ginger
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon ground allspice
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 325ml (11 fl. oz) milk

Preheat oven to 170C and prepare a 10"x13" baking tray with baking paper.

Heat treacle, syrup, sugar and margarine in a saucepan over low heat until melted. Take it off the heat and add eggs, stiring constantly.

Sieve the flour and other dry ingredients in a large bowl. Make a well in the middle, add the liquid mixture and mix.

Once all is mixed well, add the milk to it and bake for 50 minutes.
