Cod Fillet

1 Serving

  • 150-200g Cod Fillet
  • Salt and Pepper
  • 1 teaspoon margarine

Wash and dry off the fillet.

Season well with salt and pepper on both sides.

Heat the margarine over medium to high heat and put the cod in with the skin side down. Don't move it for at least 2-3 minutes.

Once the fish is nicely coloured, turn it around and leave it on medium heat for another 3-5 minutes, until the flesh turns from translucent to opaque.

Drizzle with the remaining margarine from the pan and serve with lemon juice if wished.

Serve with:

Roasted Rosemary Potatoes, Greek Stlye Green Beans, ...
