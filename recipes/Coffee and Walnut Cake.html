Coffee & Walnut Cake

7" cake

  • Cake
    • 175g selfraising flour
    • 175g margarine
    • 175g golden caster sugar
    • 3 eggs
    • 2 tbsp instant cofffe
    • 1 tbsp hot water
    • 100g walnuts (save 12 whole ones for decoration)
    • pinch of salt
  • Icing
    • 250g icing sugar
    • 75g margarine
    • 1 tbsp instant coffee
    • 1 tbsp hot water

Preheat oven to 180Â°C.

Whisk margarine and sugar very well, then add the eggs one at a time. Add the prepared coffee, then the nuts and flour.

Divide into two 7" tins and bake for 20 minutes.

In the meantime prepare the icing. Mix the sugar and margarine until very creamy, then add the coffee. Refridgerate until the cake has cooled.

Spread about half of the icing on the first layer of cake, put them togehter, then spread the rest on top. Decorate with walnuts and serve.
