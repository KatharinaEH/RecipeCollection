    • 800g Rindschnitzel salt and pepper mustard (Dijon or English) 1-2 carrots

The meat should be cut into thin steaks and tenderized, then slightly salted and peppered.

Cut somem carrot, gherkins and bacon into thing strips or mince all of it together, which makes it easier to fill the rolls.

Spread some mustard on a piece of meat, then add strips of the filling or spread some of it over the meat if minced.

Roll the beef, and if necessary secure the roll with a toothpick or some string. Roll it once in flour to lightly cover it.

If preparing for another day, freeze the rolls. This also makes it easier to fry them without them opening if you have not tied them with some string.
