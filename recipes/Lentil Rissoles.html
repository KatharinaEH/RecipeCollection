Lentil Rissoles

14 Rissoles/7 Servings

  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 125g red lentils
  • 125g green lentils
  • 125g Italian pudding rice
  • 1 can chickpeas
  • 1 slice bread, crumbled
  • 90g oats
  • 1 potato, mashed
  • 1 teaspoon thyme
  • 1 teaspoon Italian herbs
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1-2 teaspoons English Mustard
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • vegetable oil for frying

Lightly fry the onion, whilst the rice and lentils soak for a little while in water.

Rinse lentils and rice well and add to the onions. Then add chickpeas.

Cover with water and cook until tender.

Add bread crumbs.

Add oats to stiffen mixture.

Add mashed potato.

Add thyme, Italian herbs and salt then mix in mustard and crushed garlic.

Make into rectangular shape and roll in oats or breadcrumbs.

Fry on a low heat for about 20 minutes.

Serve with:

Garlic-Yoghurt Dipping Sauce

Steamed Vegetables

Mashed Potatoes

Fried White Cabbage
