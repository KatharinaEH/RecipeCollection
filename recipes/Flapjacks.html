Flapjacks

10"x12" tray

  • 8 oz margarine
  • 12 oz oats
  • 4 oz ground hazelnuts/almonds
  • 3 oz dessicated coconut
  • 4 o demerera sugar
  • 2 tbsp golden syrup (Tate&Lyle)
  • 2 oz raisins (optional)
  • 2 oz pecan pieces (optional)

Preheat oven to 150°C.

Melt margarine, sugar and golden syrup, then mix with dry goods.

Line a 10" by 12" baking tray with baking paper and bake for 30-40 minutes.
