Banana Oat Muffins

12 Servings

  • 70g melted coconut oil
  • 160g honey
  • 2 eggs
  • 5 mashed ripe bananas
  • 60g soya milk
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 200g self raising flour
  • 50g oats, plus more for sprinkling on top
  • 1 teaspoon brown sugar, for sprinkling on top

Preheat oven to 165C. If necessary, grease cups of your muffin tin with oil.

In a large bowl, beat the coconut oil and honey together with a whisk. Add eggs and beat well. Mix in the mashed bananas and milk, followed by the salt and cinnamon.

Add the flour and oats to the bowl and mix with a large spoon, just until combined. If youâ€™d like to add any additional mix-ins, like nuts or chocolate chips, fold them in now.

Divide the batter evenly between the muffin cups. Sprinkle the tops of the muffins with a small amount of oats, followed by a light sprinkling of sugar. Bake muffins for about 25 minutes.
