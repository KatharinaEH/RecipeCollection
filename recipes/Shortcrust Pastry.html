Basic Shortcrust Pastry

1 Pie Dish

  • 8 oz self-raising flour
  • 6 oz margarine
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 tablespoon water
  • 1 oz caster sugar (optional)

Weight the margarine in a mixing bowl, add a pinch of salt and sieve the flour over it.

Mix the ingredients together quickly, adding the water to bind the dough.

If the pastry is for a sweet dish, optionally add some sugar.

Wrap the dough in some cling film and keep chilled until ready to roll out for the pie form.
