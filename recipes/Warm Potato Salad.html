Warm Potato Salad

2 Servings

  • 500g new potatoes
  • 100g spinach (or other leaves)
  • 1 bunch asparagus
  • 2 tablespoons pumpkin seeds
  • 4 eggs
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons vinegar
  • salt and pepper

Preheat the oven to 200°C.

Wash and half the potatoes or if bigger, cut them into bite size peaces. Drizzle with oil and salt and mix well. Optionally add other spices, like cayenne pepper, rosemary or garlic. Spread out on a baking tray and roast for 40 min.

Wash and salt the asparagus, and after the potatoes were in the oven for 30 minutes, put the asparagus as well as the pumpking seeds onto the same tray.

Wash the spinach, then prepare a vinegraitte from 1 tablespoon of olive oil, 1 tablespoon vinegar, salt and pepper.

Boil some water, then put a tablespoon of vinegar into the water and crack the eggs into the simmering water. Poach the eggs for 4 minutes.

While the eggs are in the water, mix the spinach with the vinegar and put it on a plate. Put the roasted potatoes on top, then top with the asparagus. At last, arrange the eggs and the pumpkin seeds on the plate.
