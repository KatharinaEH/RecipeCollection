Spiced Sweet Potato Stew

8 Servings

  • 3 sweet potatoes (600g)
  • 1 red pepper
  • 2x400g cans of chopped tomatoes
  • 2 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 2 teaspoons chilli powder
  • 2 teaspoons ground cumin
  • 2 teaspoons ground coriander
  • 1 tablespoon miso paste
  • salt and pepper
  • 400g can of black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 200g spinach
  • 250g coconut yogurt (optional)

Peel the sweet potatoes, then chop them into very small (about 2 cm) pieces. Chop the red pepper into pieces of the same size.

Place the sweet potatoes and red pepper into a large saucepan with the canned tomatoes and 600ml of boiling water, then bring it all to a boil.

As it boils, add the garlic, chilli powder, cumin, coriander, miso paste, salt and pepper. Reduce the heat to a simmer, put the lid on the pan and allow it to cook for about 1 hour, making sure to stir it about every 5 minutes.

Once the sweet potatoes are soft, add the black beans to the stew with the spinach and stir well unitl the spinach has wilted totally.

If using the coconut yogurt, stir into the stew with the spinach, or serve on top.

Serve with:

Rice, quinoa, ...

Mashed avocado
