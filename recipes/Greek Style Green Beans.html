Greek Style Green Beans

2-3 Servings

  • 300g fresh or frozen green beans
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 to 1 teaspoon red chilli flakes, or to taste
  • 2 large tomatoes, roughly chopped
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/8l dry white wine
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
  • Salt and pepper

If the green beans are frozen, thaw at room temperature for about half an hour while prepping the rest of the ingredients. If fresh, wash them, cut off the ends, and half them.

Heat the olive oil over medium heat. Sauté the onion and garlic gently over medium heat for about 10 minutes, or until they are soft. Add the chilli flakes and tomatoes and sauté for another 5 minutes, until the tomato begins to break down.

Stir in the green beans, cinnamon and a bit of salt and then toss them to coat with the onion and oil. Pour in the wine and bring to a simmer over medium-high heat. Cover and cook for 40 to 50 minutes, or until the beans are tender to your liking. Season with salt and pepper to taste and serve with lemon slices, if wanted.

Serve with:

Cod Fillet, Roasted Rosemary Potatoes
