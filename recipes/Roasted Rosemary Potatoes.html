Roasted Rosemary Potatoes

2 Servings

  • 300g potatoes
  • 3 sprigs fresh rosemary (or 1 teaspoon dried rosemary)
  • salt
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

Pre-heat the oven to 200°C.

Wash and cut the potatoes into wedges or halves.

Season with rosemary and salt and drizzle with olive oil. Mix well and arrange with the skin side down on a baking tray.

Roast the potatoes for about 40-50 min, depending on size.
