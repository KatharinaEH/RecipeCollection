Pumpkin Pie

  • Pastry
    • 12 oz (340g) flour
    • 9 oz (255g) margarine
    • 1-2 tbsp water to bind
    • pinch of salt
    • 1-2 oz (30-60g) sugar (optional)
  • Filling
    • 10 oz (270g) pureed pumpkin (or steamed and mashed/blended)
    • 1/4 tsp ground ginger
    • 1/4 tsp ground nutmeg
    • pinch ground cinnamon
    • 3 eggs
    • 2 tbsp brandy
    • 1/4 pint (135 ml) milk
    • 4 oz (115g) sugar

Mix flour, margarine and salt, then add some water to bind the dough. Use 2/3 of the dough for the pie dish and keep the rest for the top.

Mix all the ingredients for the filling together, fill into the pie form and put the top on.

Bake for about 1 hour at low heat.
