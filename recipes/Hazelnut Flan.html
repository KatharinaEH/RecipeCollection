Hazelnut Flan

6 Servings

  • Shortcrust Pastry:
    • 100g margarine
    • 225g self-raising flour
    • pinch of salt
    • 2-3 tablespoons water
  • Filling:
    • 1-2 onions, depending on size
    • 200g mushrooms (optional)
    • 100g hazelnuts
    • 2-3 eggs
    • 300ml vegetable stock
    • 1 teaspoon marmite
    • salt and pepper

Put the margarine in a bowl, then sift the flour over it and mix until it forms crumbs. Add salt and a bit of water, form a dough ball and knead it briefly. Wrap the dough in cling film and chill it while preparing the filling.

Dice the onions, then sweat them over medium heat for 5-10 minutes. Slice the mushrooms and add them, keeping some to the side for decoration of the flan.

Grind the hazelnuts in a blender, then add the eggs, stock and seasoning and blend until smooth.

Before mixing in the onion, prepare the pie case.

Once the onion has cooled enough, mix with the blended mixture, then pour into flan case and arrange the rest of the mushrooms on top.

Bake at 175°C for 20 minutes, then at 150° for about 25 minutes, until the top just begins to crack and the pastry is done.

Serve with:

Parsley potatoes

Steamed kale

Cooked carrots, potatoes and peas
