Bircher Muesli

2 Servings

  • 1 apple
  • 45g rolled oats
  • 40g raisins
  • 1 tablespoon almond/peanut butter
  • 1 tablespoon chia seeds
  • 125ml soya milk
  • 1 teaspoon honey

Grate the apple into a bowl, then add all the other ingredients and stir together.

Place in an airtight jar and place in the fridge overnight (for at least 6 hours).

Serve with:

1 tablespoon coconut yoghurt

fresh berries

Source:

Deliciously Ella - Every Day - Ella Woodward
