Xiang La Chao Lian Ou (Spicy Lotus Root)

2 Servings

  • 1 fistsized piece lotus root
  • 3/4 teaspoon chili flakes
  • 1.5 tablespoon rice wine vingear
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt (to taste)

Peel and thinly slice some lotus root. Give the sliced lotus root a rinse to get some of the starch off.

Get the wok nice and hot, add a couple of tablespoons of oil, put the lotus in and stir fry for about 4 minutes. It should still be crunchy.

After the lotus root is done, add the chili flakes and the vinegar. Season with salt to taste.
