Gnocchi with Fried French Beans

2 Servings

  • 500g gnocchi
  • 2 tablespoons frying oil
  • 250g french beans
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1 teaspoon chili flakes
  • 1 teaspoon chicken powder
  • 2 tablespoons dark soy sauce

Chop the french beans into 2-3 cm long pieces and finely chop the garlic.

Heat the pan up really hot, dry-fry the beans in a some oil until they are almost cooked.

In the meantime, boil the water for the gnocchi and cook them for about 2-3 minutes.

Once the beans are done, mix in the garlic, chili flakes, chicken powder and soy sauce. After a minute, add the gnocchi to the pan and fry for a couple minutes more.
