Chickpea and Chorizo Salad

2 Servings

  • 2 cans chickpeas
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder (or smoked paprika)
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • 25g sun-dried tomatoes
  • 50g chorizo
  • 1 big carrot
  • 80g rocket
  • salt and pepper

Preheat the oven to 180Â°C. Drain the chickpeas, add the spices and olive oil and mix well. Spread them on a baking tray and roast them for about 10-15 min, shaking them once in between.

Slice the dried tomatoes and soak for 10-20 min in hot water. Slice the chorizo and carrot, heat a pan and fry the chorizo and the carrot for a couple minutes. Add the soaked tomatoes and leave on medium high heat for 2 more minutes.

Wash and chop the rocket, then add the chickpeas and the rocket to the pan. Mix well, then season with pepper and very little salt, if needed at all.
