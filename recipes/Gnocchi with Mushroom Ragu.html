Gnocchi with Mushroom Ragu

2 Servings

  • 500g gnocchi
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/2 onion
  • 400g chestnut mushrooms
  • small bunch of wild garlic
  • 5 twigs thyme
  • 100ml white wine (optional)
  • 30g parmesan

Dice the onion, then fry it in 1 tablespoon oil over low heat for about 5-10 minutes.

In the meantime, slice the mushrooms. Once done, add to the onion and season with thyme, salt and pepper. Slice the wild garlic, keeping a couple of flowers to the side for decoration, and add it towards the end of the cooking time. Cook until the liquid is gone over medium-high heat, for about 10 min. If using wine, add it when the mushrooms are nearly dry and let it evaporate as well.

After the mushrooms start cooking, heat some water for the gnocchi and cook them until done, for about 2-3 minutes. Once the mushrooms are done, add the gnocchi and a tablespoon of olive oil to the pan and fry until the gnocchi are nicely browned.

To serve, plate the gnocchi, then top with grated parmesan and some wild garlic flowers.

Serve with:

Spinach, asparagus or peas instead of wild garlic
