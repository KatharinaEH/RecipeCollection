Yellow Split Pea Dahl

6 Servings

  • Dahl:
    • 500g yellow split peas
    • 2 litres water
    • 3 vegetable stock cubes
    • 1.5 teaspoon ground turmeric
    • 2 red chillis
    • 4 slices of ginger
    • 1 cinnamon stick
  • Onion Mix:
    • 3 tablespoons vegetable oil
    • 1.5 teaspoons nigella seeds
    • 2 teaspoons cumin seeds
    • 4 cloves garlic
    • 4 cm piece of ginger
    • 2 medium to big onions
    • 1.5 teaspoons garam masala
    • 2 teaspoons ground cumin
  • juice of 1/2 lemon
  • 1/2 bunch fresh coriander leaves
s

Put the peas, water, stock cubes, turmeric, chillis (deseeded and halfed), ginger slices and cinnamon stick on high heat. Boil hard for 10 minutes, then cook for another 40-50 minutes. Mash a little bit once done.

Finely chop the garlic and ginger. Roughly chop the onion. Stir fry cumin and nigella seed in the oil on a medium to high heat for one minute. Add the garlic and ginger and cook for another minute, then add the onion.

Keep cooking on medium heat until the dahl is almost done, then turn the heat up and add the garam masala and ground cumin. Heat for 2 minutes, then add to the dahl, after mashing the dahl.

Add the lemon juice and mix in chopped coriander leaves, leaving some for garnish.

Serve with:

Boiled baby potatoes

Stir-fried spinach

Naan bread
