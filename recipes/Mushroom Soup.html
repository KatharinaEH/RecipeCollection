Mushroom Soup

2-3 Servings

  • 250g chestnut mushrooms
  • 1 big onion
  • 3 spring onions (optional)
  • 3-4 cloves garlic
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 3 sprigs of thyme
  • 1 vegetable or chicken stock cube
  • 1 can conconut milk
  • salt and pepper

Chop the onions and garlic roughly, then fry on medium heat in a bit of oil.

Chop the mushrooms, then add them and the thyme to the onion, put the lid on and keep frying for 5 minutes.

Once the mushrooms are done, add the coconut milk and stockcube, bring to a boil and blend with an immersion blender. Season with pepper and salt, if necessary.
