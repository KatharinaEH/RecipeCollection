Chocolate Mousse with Spicy Watermelon

6 Servings

  • Chocolate Mousse:
    • 250g dark chocolate (optionally orange or chili flavoured)
    • 1 egg
    • 1 pinch salt
    • 2 tablespoons orange liquor (optional)
    • 500ml cream, lightly whipped
    • fresh mint for garnish (optional)
  • Spicy Watermelon:
    • 1/2 teaspoon chili flakes
    • 40g honey
    • 1 teaspoon vinegar
    • 2 tablespoons olive oil
    • 500g watermelon

Melt the chocolate. Whip the egg and a pinch of salt over the double boiler on low heat, until it doubles in size and reaches around 70C.

If using, add the orange liquor to the egg, then add the chocolate and mix well.

Mix in the cream carefully, and fill into glasses or bowls. Cool for at least 4 hours.

Cut the melon into bite-sized cubes. Bring 50ml water with the chili, honey, vinegar and olive oil to a boil. Boil for a minute, then pour over the melon, mix and leave in fridge to marinade for at least 4 hours.
